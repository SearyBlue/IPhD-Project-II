\paragraph{2.}
The \textbf{only change} in the Kondo cloud effective Hamiltonian is interchanging the \il{s^+} and \il{s^-}. That should give
\beq
H_\text{eff} = \fr{J^*}{2}s_z + J^*s^-s^+ = \fr{J^*}{2}s^z + J^*\rr{\fr{1}{2} - s^z} = +\fr{3}{4}J^*
\eeq
after putting \il{s^z = -\fr{1}{2}}. This is different from the \il{-\fr{3}{4}J^*} you mentioned in the docs. You said "The resulting expression will have one sign change". But I can't see such a sign change.

\pagebreak
The equations are
\beq
a_1\rr{H_0^* - \fr{J^*}{2}s^z}\ket{\phi_1} + a_0 \fr{J^*}{2}s^+\ket{\phi_0} = a_1 E \ket{\phi_1}\\
a_0\rr{H_0^* + \fr{J^*}{2}s^z}\ket{\phi_0} + a_1 \fr{J^*}{2}s^-\ket{\phi_1} = a_0 E \ket{\phi_0}\\
\eeq
From the first equation, 
\beq
\ket{\phi_1} = \fr{a_0}{a_1}\fr{J^*}{2}\qq{E - H_0^* + \fr{J^*}{2}s^z}^{-1}s^+\ket{\phi_0}
\eeq
Substituting this into the second equation,
\begin{gather}
a_0\rr{H_0^* + \fr{J^*}{2}s^z}\ket{\phi_0} + a_0 \rr{\fr{J^*}{2}}^2 s^-\qq{E - H_0^* + \fr{J^*}{2}s^z}^{-1}s^+\ket{\phi_0} = a_0 E \ket{\phi_0}\\
\implies \rr{E - H_0^*}\ket{\phi_0} = \fr{J^*}{2}s^z\ket{\phi_0} + \rr{\fr{J^*}{2}}^2s^-\fr{1}{E - H_0^* + \fr{J^*}{2}s^z}s^+\ket{\phi_0}
\end{gather}
The effective Hamiltonian can be read off:
\beq
H_\text{eff} &= \fr{J^*}{2}s^z + \rr{\fr{J^*}{2}}^2s^-\fr{1}{E - H_0^* + \fr{J^*}{2}s^z}s^+\\
&\sim \fr{J^*}{2}s^z + \rr{\fr{J^*}{2}}^2s^-\fr{1}{E + \fr{J^*}{2}s^z}s^+\\
&\sim \fr{J^*}{2}s^z + \rr{\fr{J^*}{2}}^2s^-\fr{1}{\fr{J^*}{2}s^z}s^+ &&\qq{E=0}\\
&\sim \fr{J^*}{2}s^z + \fr{J^*}{2}s^-s^+ \times 2 &&\qq{\fr{1}{s^z}s^+ = 2s^+}\\
&\sim \fr{J^*}{2}s^z + J^*\rr{\fr{1}{2} - s^z}
\eeq
